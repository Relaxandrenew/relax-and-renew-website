Genuine Care for Everyone

At Relax & Renew RMT, personal wellness is our priority. We founded this service with a commitment to bring professional, compassionate care directly to those who need it most—especially seniors and individuals with mobility challenges—while remaining fully available and welcoming to everyone in our community.

We move beyond the standard "in-and-out" clinic experience. Our therapists are dedicated to understanding your unique story, creating individualized treatment plans, and providing tailored aftercare designed to support your long-term health goals. We bring the sanctuary of healing into the comfort and safety of your own home.

Cassandra Simpson

Registered Massage Therapist & Co-Founder

As a Registered Massage Therapist since 2016, I have had the pleasure of working in diverse settings, including clinics, spas, long-term care homes, and various events. I am now providing mobile massage services to bring the sanctuary directly to you!

My commitment is to provide the highest level of care to support my clients on each of their unique wellness journeys. I am passionate about helping clients achieve and maintain their individual health goals through tailored treatment plans and continued care.
