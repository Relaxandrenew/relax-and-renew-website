Transparency & Value

How It Works

Our membership is built on a simple monthly credit system. There are no hidden fees or signup costs—just consistent care at a preferred rate.

1. Monthly Credits

Your monthly payment purchases a treatment credit. This credit sits in your account until you're ready to use it.

2. Flexible Rollovers

Busy month? No problem. Unused credits roll over automatically and never expire while your membership is active.

3. Shareable Care

Credits are transferable within your household. You can also stack credits for longer treatments (e.g., a 2-hour massage).
